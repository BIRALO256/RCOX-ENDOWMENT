Luduk's Journey

Luduk at Home

This picture shows Luduk at home, where he often spends his time after school. Despite having his school fees paid by the organization, Luduk sometimes has to collect firewood in the bush to earn money for books. This glimpse into his daily life highlights the challenges he faces.

Luduk at School

This picture shows Luduk(in the blue shirt) at school on the day our organization visited ,Luduk received books, and other children also received water bottles, bags, and other scholastic materials to support their education journey.

Thank You Message from Headmaster

Watch the heartfelt message from Luduk's school headmaster expressing gratitude for the support provided by our organization.As we continue to assist children like Luduk, your support in various ways can make a significant impact. Explore how you can contribute and make a difference today

Queen, Ashemma, Eric, and Bright

Meet Queen, Ashemma, Eric, and Bright. Left by their mother and with their father passed on, they faced hardships and were forced to drop out of school. With the support of our organization, they now have a stable home and are back in school. You can help them continue to improve their situation.

Queen

Here is Queen, one of the children, in her school uniform. With the support of our organization, she is now back in school and has the opportunity to pursue her education. Your contribution can make a difference in their lives.
